What we do

Dirt work, done right.

From a new driveway to a full site prep for your cabin or addition. We handle excavation, septic, and earthwork across the UP.

⛰

Site Preparation

Clearing, grading, foundation excavation. We get your site ready for your builder so the project doesn't stall waiting on dirt work.

🚽

Septic Install & Repair

New systems, replacements, drain field repairs. Permitted, inspected, and built to last UP winters. We handle the paperwork.

🛣

Driveways & Roads

Gravel, paved, or two-track. We grade, base-prep, and finish. Camp roads through hard terrain are our specialty.

🌲

Land Clearing

Hunting camps, building sites, view-clearing for cabins. We take down trees, grind stumps, and haul off the brush.

🏗

Demolition

Old cabins, sheds, garages, foundations. We knock it down, haul it off, and grade the site clean.

💧

Drainage & Erosion

Water in your basement? Yard washing out? We fix the grade, install French drains, and stop the problem at the source.
