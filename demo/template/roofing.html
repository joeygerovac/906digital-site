What we do

Built for UP weather. Backed by warranty.

UP roofs take a beating — heavy snow loads, ice dams, lake-effect winds, summer storms. We build for what the weather actually does here, not for textbook conditions.

Residential Roofing

Asphalt, metal, cedar, slate. Full tear-offs, partial replacements, and repairs. 50-year material options on every install.

⚡

Storm Damage & Insurance

Wind, hail, ice damage. We document the damage, work with your adjuster, and get the claim through. Most clients pay only their deductible.

❄

Ice Dam Removal

UP winters cause ice dams that destroy roofs from the top down. We remove them safely without damaging shingles, and fix the cause.
